Why PageAgent

What took 20 clicks now takes one sentence.

Turn any SaaS into an AI copilot in minutes. Drop-in, dependency-free, and ready for production.

🎯

Precise on-page actions

Finds and clicks, fills, selects & scrolls real DOM elements by meaning — no screenshots, no vision model needed.

🧠

Bring your own LLM

Works zero-config with a built-in planner. Connect Qwen, GPT, Claude or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint for full NL.

📝

Smart form filling

One sentence fills entire ERP / CRM / admin forms correctly, field by field.

🎙️

Voice & accessibility

Built-in speech-to-text and screen-reader announcements. Control the app without touching the mouse.

🔌

MCP-ready & extensible

Event-driven API (on/emit) so external tools, Chrome extensions or MCP servers can drive it.

🪶

// react to every step
PageAgent.on('step', e => log(e));
PageAgent.on('done', e => done(e));
